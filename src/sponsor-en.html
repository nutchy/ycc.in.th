What is YCC?

Young Creator's Camp is a camp that brings high school students who have some programming or design knowledge to create a website or application prototype within three days.

The concept is that, we build solutions to solve the problems we encounter in everyday life, to make our lives more comfortable. But what does it take from our code to become a Software Product that can solve the problems of society?

We will focus on Product Development Mindset (Code to Create, Code with Purpose), and things we’ll teach include Design Thinking, Lean Validation, UX Fundamentals and Agile & Scrum.

Why YCC needs sponsors?

This event is organized by young volunteers who wanted to organize activities that brings children to learn about making Software Products. We don’t receive any compensation from organizing this camp. There are various expenses in organizing events like this, such as food for children and staff, travel expenses, accommodation fees and many more. Most staff are still studying in high school or undergraduate universities. Without support by various agencies, we would not be able to make this camp a reality.

The organizing team would like to thank you for your kind support, taking part in creating a society of giving opportunities for the younger generation to learn the skills to build products and create a good future for Thai society.
