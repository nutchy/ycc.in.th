What is YCC?

Young Creator's Camp is a product development camp that challenges high-school student developers and designers to join forces and unleash their creativity, and develop a software product to alleviate the pain points of the Thai communities within three days.

The student will be participating in our exclusive three-day workshop that blends ideation, idea validation, and product creation. They'll learn the fundamental principles of Design Thinking, Lean Startup, User Experience and Agile & Scrum, in a way that has never been seen before.

The key idea is this: As developers and designers, we build solutions and designs to solve the problems we encounter in everyday life, to make our lives more comfortable. However, what does it take for our code to become a software product that can enhance people's daily lives?

Why YCC needs sponsors?

This event is organized by the young volunteers who wanted to create activities that brings children to learn about making software products. We did not receive any compensation from organizing this camp.

There are various expenses in organizing events like this, such as food for children and staff, travel expenses, accommodation fees and many more.

Most of our team members are still studying in high school or undergraduate universities. Without support by various agencies, we would not be able to make this camp a reality.

The organizing team would like to thank you for your kind support, taking part in creating a society of giving opportunities for the younger generation to learn the skills to build products and create a good future for the Thai society.
